We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). However, we may collect:

  • Game Progress & High Scores: Stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Non-personal details like device model, OS, and screen resolution for performance improvements.
  • Analytics (if enabled): Data on gameplay sessions to enhance game features.

2. Third-Party Services

If the game includes ads, leaderboards, or analytics, third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)

Please refer to their privacy policies for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay experience and optimize performance.
  • Fix bugs and crashes.
  • Track high scores and game progress.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your game progress is stored **locally** on your device. If cloud saving is enabled (Google Play Games, Apple Game Center), your data may be stored on those platforms.

5. Children's Privacy

This game is for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under **13 years old**.

6. User Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of analytics if included.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.
