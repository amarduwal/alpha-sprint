Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Welcome to Alpha Sprint! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Game Progress & High Scores: Stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Non-personal details like device model, OS, and screen resolution for performance improvements.
  • Analytics (if enabled): Data on gameplay sessions to enhance game features.

2. Third-Party Services

If the game includes ads, leaderboards, or analytics, third-party services may collect data:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)
  • Google Play Services (for leaderboards)
  • Firebase Analytics (for tracking game statistics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve gameplay experience and optimize performance.
  • Fix bugs and crashes.
  • Track high scores and game progress.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your game progress is stored **locally** on your device. If cloud saving is enabled, your data may be stored on those platforms.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under **13 years old**.

6. User Rights & Control

  • Reset game data (via game settings).
  • Opt out of analytics if included.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.
